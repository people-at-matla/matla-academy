The Mission

Why This Programme Exists

South Africa has one of the most underserved financial advisory markets in the world. Millions of families lack access to qualified advisors who understand their lives, their language, and their needs. Matla Academy was built to close that gap — by investing in people like you.

The JFA (Junior Financial Advisor) to FA Accelerator is a fast-tracked, mentorship-backed training programme. You were selected because Matla Life Insurance Group saw potential in you. This programme is your bridge from where you are to where you deserve to be — a credentialed, earning, impactful Financial Advisor.

The Real Talk

You Were Chosen.
Now Choose to Show Up.

This opportunity is rare. Most people spend years trying to get into financial services. You have a structured programme, a supportive team, and a guaranteed path — if you commit. Do not take that for granted.
Your community needs you qualified. There is a chronic shortage of Black financial advisors in South Africa. When you succeed, you don't just change your life — you become accessible expertise for people who've never had it before. That is power.
Your income has no ceiling. Financial advisors earn based on the value they deliver. The more clients you serve, the more policies you place, the more protection you provide — the more you earn. This is not a job. It's a business you build inside an established brand.
Every module you skip is a client you'll lose. The assessment tests everything. FAIS, sales ethics, product structures, financial planning — all of it is examined. Advisors who cut corners on training get caught. The ones who master the material get trusted, get referrals, and get rich.
Failing is recoverable. Quitting is not. If you don't pass on the first attempt, the programme has a retake path. But if you disengage, stop showing up, or stop caring — that door closes. The people who succeed are rarely the smartest. They are the most consistent.
